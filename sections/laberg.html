Laberg

My great-grandpa, Søren Urnes, took his house in 1922 and moved from Bakken in Jostedalen to Ytre Eikjo, where he got from his father-in-law the farm Laberg ². That was one of the farms that origin from the original farm Ytre Eikjo.

Søren had many children with him. So they started up again whit school in Eikjastrondi ⁵. Electricity come to Laberg in the first half of the 1950s ¹.
